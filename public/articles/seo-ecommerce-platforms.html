How SEO Across 7 Platforms Added Free Revenue to Our D2C Brand

3.2× Organic Impressions 6 min read SEO · D2C

Most D2C brands think SEO is just for Google. It isn't. At TruNativ, we treated every marketplace as a search engine. Optimising listings across Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, and 4 other platforms built a compounding organic revenue stream that complemented our paid campaigns perfectly.

3.2×
Increase in total organic impressions across 7 marketplace platforms in 6 months

The Multi-Platform SEO Strategy

Each platform has its own algorithm, but they all share one goal: show the product most likely to convert for a given search query. Our strategy was to dominate the "relevance" and "conversion" signals for our core keywords across all 7 platforms.

"SEO on marketplaces is 50% keyword relevance and 50% conversion velocity. You can't rank for a keyword you don't convert on."

The 7 Platforms

  • Amazon: The gold standard. Focus on A+ content, backend keywords, and review velocity.
  • Flipkart: High volume, price-sensitive. Focus on title optimisation and category-specific attributes.
  • Meesho: Rapidly growing. Focus on simple, benefit-first titles and high-quality primary images.
  • Nykaa: Beauty/Wellness niche. Focus on ingredient-led descriptions and expert reviews.
  • Myntra: Lifestyle focus. Focus on lifestyle imagery and trend-led keywords.
  • BigBasket / Blinkit: Quick commerce. Focus on availability and high-frequency purchase keywords.
  • Shopify (Direct): Traditional Google SEO. Focus on technical health, content clusters, and backlink profile.

The Optimisation Framework

  • Keyword Research: Use platform-specific tools (like Helium 10 for Amazon) to find what people actually type into the search bar.
  • Title Optimisation: Lead with the highest-volume keyword + primary benefit + brand name.
  • Bullet Points: Focus on benefits, not features. Use capitalised headers for readability.
  • A+ Content / Enhanced Brand Content: Use visuals to handle objections and show the product in use.
  • Backend Keywords: Fill every available character with synonyms and related terms that don't fit in the public listing.

Marketplace SEO

Focus on conversion velocity, review count, and platform-specific keyword fields. Results are faster.

Google SEO

Focus on technical health, content authority, and backlinks. Results are slower but more defensive.

The Results

By treating these 7 platforms as search engines rather than just sales channels, we increased total organic impressions by 3.2× in 6 months. This created a "free" revenue stream that improved our overall MER (Marketing Efficiency Ratio) and reduced our dependence on paid Meta and Google ads.
