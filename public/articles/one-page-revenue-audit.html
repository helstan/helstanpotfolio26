The One-Page Audit That Found ₹40L in Leaking Revenue

-28% Drop-off 6 min read CRO

Most brands I've worked with aren't losing revenue from bad targeting. They're losing it from friction between the click and the purchase. This one-page audit framework identified ₹40 lakhs in monthly revenue leakage in a single 90-minute session.

Why Revenue Audits Beat Creative Testing

A 10% improvement in conversion rate is worth more than a 10% reduction in CPA. If you're spending ₹10L/month and converting at 2%, fixing conversion to 2.5% gives you 25% more revenue from the same spend — without touching your ads.

Yet most brands spend all their energy optimising the ad side and ignore the leaky bucket on the other end.

"Don't pour more water into a leaky bucket. Fix the hole first — then scale the pour."

The One-Page Audit Framework

This framework covers five zones. For each, you need: a heatmap tool (Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity), GA4 funnel reports, and your ad account data.

Zone 1 — Landing Page First Fold

  • Is the headline benefit-first or feature-first? Benefit-first wins
  • Does the CTA appear above the fold on mobile without scrolling?
  • Is page load under 3 seconds? Every second adds ~7% drop-off
  • Is there a trust signal (reviews, ratings, press logos) in the first fold?

Zone 2 — Add to Cart Drop-off

  • Check GA4: what % of product page viewers click Add to Cart?
  • Is there a strong reason to buy NOW (scarcity, offer, urgency)?
  • Are product images showing use-case, not just product on white?
  • Is the price anchored against a higher original price or competitor?

Zone 3 — Checkout Friction

  • How many form fields are in the checkout? Every extra field loses ~3–5% of buyers
  • Is guest checkout available? Forcing account creation kills 22% of buyers on average
  • Are all popular payment methods available (UPI, cards, wallets, EMI)?
  • Is there a progress bar so buyers know how many steps remain?
22%
Average buyer loss rate when account creation is forced at checkout

Zone 4 — Post-Click Attribution

  • Are your Meta Pixel and GA4 sending the same conversion events?
  • Is server-side tracking set up? iOS changes make pixel-only data unreliable
  • Are you attributing correctly — last-click vs. data-driven attribution models?

Zone 5 — Post-Purchase Revenue

  • Is there an upsell or cross-sell after purchase?
  • Is there a thank-you page email opt-in flow?
  • Are abandoned cart sequences running within 30 minutes of abandonment?

What the ₹40L Audit Found

For the D2C brand where this framework uncovered ₹40L in monthly leakage, the three biggest issues were: a 6-second mobile page load time, forced account creation at checkout, and zero post-purchase upsell sequence. Fixing these over 6 weeks reduced drop-off by 28% and added ₹40L in monthly revenue from the same ad spend.
